My Hunting Story

For over twenty years, the rhythm of the season has dictated my year. It's not just about the harvest; it’s about the stillness before dawn, the meticulous planning, and the deep respect for the land and the game. My first hunt was with my grandfather in the crisp Minnesota air, and that connection to the wild has only deepened. This site is a record of those experiences.

From gear selection to tracking strategies in challenging weather, I aim to provide genuine, tested advice. The pursuit of the perfect buck is a lifelong endeavor, and every season teaches me something new. I believe in ethical hunting, conservation, and passing on the tradition.

— Scott Rindahl, Dedicated Outdoorsman
